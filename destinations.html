Torqauy

A week in the Mediterranean - and you didn’t even have to fly in a plane or sail in a cruise ship! Torquay is the most glamourous of all West Country resorts, boasting an international flavour – its palm trees, luxury yachts and bustling harbor really will leave you feeling like you’re on a beach in the med! It offers all of the beauty of a holiday abroad, without having to book an air holiday and worry about your passport – just board a coach and away you go.

Eastbourne

Head to the UK’s sunniest destination for a holiday with endless blue skies. Bask in the warm sunshine on the golden beach, stroll along the pier and pop some pennies into traditional arcade games and wander the colourful carpet gardens! You get the best of both worlds with Eastbourne, with the sweeping South Downs Countryside on the doorstep, leaving you with plenty to explore.

Queen of Welsh resorts! Llandudno boasts all the fun and entertainment of a traditional seaside holiday, with attractions by the score.
